Spaghetti

Description

This simple spaghetti recipe will keep your family happy for years. Make the sauce ahead, or double it and freeze the leftovers for a quick dinner night later. Serves 4 and takes 45 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (about 2 teaspoons)
  • 6 ounces Italian sweet sausage (1 link)
  • 6 ounces Italina hot sausage (1 link)
  • 1 28-oz. can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • salt
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet on medium or medium high heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until transclucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook 1 minute more.
  4. While the onions are cooking, put a large pot of salted water on to boil for the pasta.
  5. Removed the cooked onion and garlic from the pan and set aside.
  6. Remove the sausage from the casings and add to the pan, breaking up the meat as you go. Cook on medium heat until just lightly browned.
  7. Add the crushed tomatoes to the skillet with the meat, and stir in the cooked onions and garlic.
  8. Heat just until simmering.
  9. Once the pasta water has come to a rolling boil, add the spaghetti.
  10. Once the water has returned to boiling, cook, uncovered, for 10-12 minutes.
  11. Remove pasta pot from heat, and drain pasta before placing in a large serving bowl.
  12. Toss pasta with sauce and serve immediately.
  13. Garnish with Parmesan cheese.
